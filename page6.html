LOUCHE

a duo I form
together with Maily Beyrens, 
We do strange concerts,
make active constellations with objects we appreciate and ourselves,
together with you we perform cheesechoirs. 
For every show we make a personal score in advance.
We never did the same show twice, maybe one day we will
but we like to adapt one show to a particular event in a specific place.
We work with all kind of improfessional instruments that fit our state of mind.
This collaboration gives me a lot of energy and we vibrate on a similar wave 
that feeds our inspiration together.
